About Me

Composer | Record Producer

Growing up as a classically trained musician, F!tz soon developed an interest in a variety of musical styles and genres. As a teenager, he joined numerous rock bands and pursued jazz studies in his undergraduate years at a Music Conservatory in Italy.


After his studies, he moved to London in 2012 to undertake a Master's Degree in Music Composition for Film and TV. Since then, he has worked on a multitude of film, theatre, TV and record productions with both established names in the industry and up-and-coming filmmakers and artists.


In 2016, he joined Soho Sonic Studios, an award winning Music Production Studio in Central London as a Senior Music Producer and Sound Engineer. His job in the studio requires him to be proficient in all styles of music and he can combine multiple influences effortlessly into your track. He is also a multi-instrumentalist and a skilled vocal producer.


Alongside his studio activities, he continues to work as a freelance composer and record producer on numerous exciting projects, writing and producing in a wide range of musical styles.

My Services

One-stop Shop Approach

With more than 5 years of professional experience and numerous films, commercials and video games under his belt, F!tz brings a wealth of knowledge and can transform your picture with beautiful and evocative scores. Although his music is unique, it is influenced by neo-classical and ambient pieces.

He can spot a project in-person or remotely, listen to your ideas and become part of your vision. He can help you bring your story to life with unique ideas, or by recreating existing works in the public domain. Final stems for post production are delivered in a timely manner, and all scores are prepared for live recording.
