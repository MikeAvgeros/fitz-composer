About Me

Composer | Record Producer

Growing up as a classically trained musician, Fitz soon developed an interest in a variety of musical styles and genres. As a teenager, he joined numerous rock bands and pursued jazz studies in his undergraduate years at a Music Conservatory in Italy.


After his studies, he moved to London in 2012 to undertake a Master's Degree in Music Composition for Film and TV. Since then, he has worked on a multitude of film, theatre, TV and record productions with both established names in the industry and up-and-coming filmmakers and artists.


In 2016, he joined an award winning Music Production Studio in Central London as a Senior Music Producer and Sound Engineer. Alongside his studio activities, he continues to work as a freelancer on numerous projects, writing and producing in a wide range of musical styles.
