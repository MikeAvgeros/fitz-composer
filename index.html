Francesco Marseglia

Film Composer | Record Producer

Growing up as a classically trained musician and composer, Francesco soon developed an interest in a variety of music styles and genres. He joined numerous rock bands as a teenager and pursued jazz studies in his undergraduate years at a Music Conservatory in Italy.


After his studies, he moved to London in 2012 to undertake a Master's Degree in Music Composition for Film and TV. Since then, he has worked on a multitude of film, theatre, TV and song productions with both established names in the industry and up-and-coming filmmakers and artists.


In early 2016, he joined an award winning Music Production Studio in Central London as a Senior Music Producer and Sound Engineer. Alongside his studio activities, he continues to work as a freelancer on various projects, writing and producing in a wide range of musical styles.
